# AWS Certified Generative AI Developer - Professional (AIP-C01)

## Exam at a Glance

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Exam Code** | AIP-C01 |
| **Duration** | 180 minutes (3 hours) |
| **Questions** | 75 (65 scored + 10 unscored) |
| **Passing Score** | 750 / 1000 |
| **Cost** | $300 USD |
| **Format** | Multiple choice & multiple response |
| **Languages** | English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese |
| **Testing** | Pearson VUE center or online proctored |

## Domain Weightings

| Domain | Weight | Focus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **D1: FM Integration, Data Management & Compliance** | **31%** | RAG, vector stores, prompt engineering, FM selection |
| **D2: Implementation & Integration** | **26%** | Agents, deployment, enterprise integration, APIs |
| **D3: AI Safety, Security & Governance** | **20%** | Guardrails, PII, compliance, responsible AI |
| **D4: Operational Efficiency & Optimization** | **12%** | Cost optimization, performance, monitoring |
| **D5: Testing, Validation & Troubleshooting** | **11%** | Evaluation, debugging, quality assurance |

## Prerequisites (Recommended)

- 2+ years building production apps on AWS
- 1+ year hands-on implementing GenAI solutions
- General AI/ML or data engineering experience
- AWS compute, storage, networking, security knowledge

## Scoring Model

- **Compensatory** - no need to pass individual domains
- **No penalty** for guessing - answer everything!
- 10 unscored questions mixed in (you can't tell which)

## Retake Policy

- Wait **14 days** if you fail
- No limit on attempts
- Full fee ($300) per attempt
- Cancel/reschedule free up to **24 hours** before

## What's NOT on the Exam

- Model development and training from scratch
- Advanced ML techniques (gradient descent, backprop details)
- Data engineering and feature engineering
- Amazon Redshift, AWS Budgets, IoT services, etc.

## 3-Day Study Plan

### Day 1: Domains 1 & 2 (57% of exam)
- Morning: Domain 1 - FM Integration, RAG, Vector Stores, Prompt Engineering
- Afternoon: Domain 2 - Agents, Deployment, Enterprise Integration, APIs
- Evening: Review Amazon Bedrock docs + practice questions

### Day 2: Domains 3, 4 & 5 (43% of exam)
- Morning: Domain 3 - Safety, Security, Guardrails, Responsible AI
- Afternoon: Domain 4 - Cost optimization, Performance, Monitoring
- Evening: Domain 5 - Testing, Evaluation, Troubleshooting

### Day 3: Review & Practice
- Morning: Services cheat sheet + weak areas
- Afternoon: Practice exam questions
- Evening: Light review of key concepts, rest well
